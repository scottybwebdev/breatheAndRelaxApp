About

The Simple Breathing Techniques app offers various levels of simple breathing techniques to help you relax and de-stress. Instructions for each individual breathing technique can be found within their section. All exercises are intended to last for up to 5 minutes but you can stop beforehand if you wish.

The recommended web browser for the time being is Google Chrome.

Whilst building this app, I used numerous resources and combined them together to create this app. The resources I accessed can be found below under the References heading.
