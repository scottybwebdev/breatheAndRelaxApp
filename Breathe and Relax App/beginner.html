Calming Breathing Technique

  • Let your breath flow as deep down into your belly as is comfortable, without forcing it.
  • Try breathing in through your nose and out through your mouth.
  1. Breathe in gently and regularly. Some people find it helpful to count steadily from 1 to 5. You may not be able to reach 5 at first.
  2. Then, without pausing or holding your breath, let it flow out gently, counting from 1 to 5 again, if you find this helpful.
  3. Keep doing this for 3 to 5 minutes.
